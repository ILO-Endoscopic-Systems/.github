# ILO Electronic GmbH

ILO Electronic GmbH is a German manufacturer specializing in medical technology for minimally invasive surgery, with a strong focus on endoscopy systems. Our headquarters are located in Northern Germany near Hamburg.  

With a team of approximately 40 professionals across development, manufacturing, sales, and administration, we combine engineering expertise with practical clinical insight to deliver reliable and innovative medical solutions.

---

## 🏥 Experience

**Medical Devices Since 1993**

For more than 30 years, ILO Electronic GmbH has been a trusted OEM partner for international medical brands.  

We design and manufacture a complete portfolio of endoscopy equipment that meets the evolving medical, technological, and regulatory requirements of global markets.  

Through continuous product development and close collaboration with our customers, we ensure that our systems remain aligned with clinical practice and industry standards.

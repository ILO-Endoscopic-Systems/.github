# ILO Electronic GmbH

ILO electronic GmbH is a manufacturer in the field of minimally invasive surgery with a focus on endoscopy systems. Our headquarter is located in the north of Germany near Hamburg. At present our staff comprises about 40 employees in the development, sales, manufacturing and administration sectors.

---

## Experience

**Medical devices since 1993**

For over 30 years, ILO electronic GmbH has been a reliable partner for various brands in the OEM sector and sells a complete line of equipment. Customers from all over the world appreciate the benefits of our flexible portfolio of endoscopy devices. Through ongoing development and close contact with our customers, we are close to the medical market, keeping our products up to date with medical, technological and functional requirements.

---

## Customization

As an OEM service partner we offer customized solutions for our whole product range. Many technical and functional components can be adapted according to your needs. Besides the option to choose our standard equipment, the interface design and housing of devices can also be customized to match the retailer's own corporate identity. This modular customization concept allows us to offer an appropriate supply responding to our clients' demand.

## Quality

**Standard acc. to EN ISO 13485**

A high quality standard and excellent service have always been – and are still – essential parts of our company policy right from the start and have certainly contributed a great deal to the success of ILO electronic GmbH.
